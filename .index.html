Privacy Policy for Disco Light Marquee

Last updated: May 3, 2025

Ganjooro Interactive ("We", "Us", or "Our") operates the Disco Light Marquee app (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how We collect, use, and share information when You use the App. By using the App, You agree to this policy.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as Your name, email, or location. The App collects the following data:

  • Permissions:
    • Camera: Used only to control the flashlight. No images or videos are captured or stored.
    • Microphone: Used only for Beat Detection to sync lights with sound. Audio is processed locally, not stored or sent.
  • Usage Data: Collected by Google AdMob and Google Play Billing, including device info and app interactions, for ads and analytics.
  • Advertising Data: In the free version, Google AdMob uses Your Advertising ID to show ads. You can adjust this in Your device settings.
  • Billing Data: For in-app purchases, Google Play Billing handles transactions. We don’t access payment details.
  • Local Storage: Preferences (e.g., marquee settings) are saved on Your device using SharedPreferences.

How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Provide App features like flashlight effects and marquee display.
  • Manage in-app purchases and remove ads for premium users.
  • Show ads in the free version via Google AdMob.
  • Improve the App using aggregated usage data.

Data Sharing

We share data with:

  • Google: For ads (AdMob) and billing (Google Play Billing).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law.

No other sharing occurs.

Data Retention

We don’t retain PII. Usage data retention follows Google’s policies. Local preferences stay on Your device until the App is uninstalled.

Data Security

We rely on Android’s security features. Local data isn’t encrypted.

Children’s Privacy

The App isn’t for children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect data from them.

Third-Party Links

The App may link to external sites (e.g., Google policies). We’re not responsible for their practices.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy and notify You in the App. Check back periodically.
